Ravenclaw House

"Or yet in wise old Ravenclaw, if you've a ready mind, where those of wit and learning will always find their kind."

Founder

Rowena Ravenclaw

One of the four founders of Hogwarts, Rowena Ravenclaw prized intelligence, knowledge, curiosity, creativity and wit above all else.

House Ghost

The Grey Lady

The Grey Lady is the Ravenclaw house ghost. She is the daughter of Rowena Ravenclaw and was killed by the Bloody Baron who later became the Slytherin ghost.
