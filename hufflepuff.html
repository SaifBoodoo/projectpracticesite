Hufflepuff House

"You might belong in Hufflepuff, where they are just and loyal, those patient Hufflepuffs are true and unafraid of toil."

Founder

Helga Hufflepuff

One of the four founders of Hogwarts, Helga Hufflepuff valued hard work, patience, loyalty, and fair play. She accepted all students without bias.

House Ghost

The Fat Friar

The Fat Friar is the friendly Hufflepuff ghost who was executed because senior churchmen grew suspicious of his ability to cure the pox merely by poking peasants with a stick.
