Founder

Salazar Slytherin

One of the four founders of Hogwarts, Salazar Slytherin valued ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. He believed only pure-blood wizards should attend Hogwarts.

House Ghost

The Bloody Baron

The Bloody Baron is the Slytherin house ghost. He is covered in silver blood stains and is the only one who can control Peeves. He killed the Grey Lady (Ravenclaw's ghost) when she rejected his love.
