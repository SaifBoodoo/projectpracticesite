"You might belong in Gryffindor, where dwell the brave at heart, their daring, nerve, and chivalry set Gryffindors apart."

Founder

Godric Gryffindor

One of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Godric Gryffindor prized courage, determination and strength of heart above all other qualities.

House Ghost

Nearly Headless Nick

Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington, known as Nearly Headless Nick, is the Gryffindor house ghost. He was beheaded but the axe was blunt so it didn't fully go through his neck.
